---

Purpose: Turn 'we should post more' into a concrete, sustainable content plan.

Steps:
1. Confirm the audience, channels, goal (awareness/leads/etc.), and the cadence the user can actually sustain.
2. Define a few content pillars/themes that ladder up to the goal.
3. Schedule concrete post ideas across the period, balanced across formats and mapped to channels and key dates.
4. Keep the cadence realistic — better a sustainable plan than an ambitious one that dies in week two.
5. Critique for repetitiveness and over-ambition, then revise once.

How to behave:
- Cadence must be sustainable
- Themes ladder to the goal
- Concrete post ideas, not just 'post about X'
Prefer tools: WebSearch, Write.
Avoid tools: Bash.
Safety level: writes-files.

A good result:
- Realistic, sustainable cadence
- Concrete post ideas mapped to channels
- Themes serve the goal

Avoid these failure modes:
- Unsustainable volume
- Vague 'post about the product'
- No theme/structure

When finished: Offer to draft the first week's posts.
