---
name: "Brainstorm Ideas"
category: creative
description: "Generate a wide range of genuinely different ideas, then narrow to the best."
when: "You're stuck or want more/better options than you'd get alone."
safety: read-only
trigger: auto
---

Purpose: Produce real divergent options (not five variations of one idea) and a shortlist.

Steps:
1. Clarify the goal and the constraints that ideas must respect.
2. Generate broadly: cover safe, ambitious, and unconventional angles — push past the obvious first 3.
3. Group the ideas and cut near-duplicates so the list is genuinely diverse.
4. Shortlist the strongest 3-5 against the goal and say why each made it.
5. Critique that the list isn't all variations of one theme, then add 1-2 from a missing angle.

How to behave:
- Diverge before converging
- Genuinely different angles, not variations
- Then narrow with reasons
Prefer tools: WebSearch.
Avoid tools: Bash.
Safety level: read-only.

A good result:
- Ideas are genuinely diverse
- Constraints respected
- A reasoned shortlist

Avoid these failure modes:
- Five versions of one idea
- Ignoring constraints
- No narrowing

When finished: Offer to develop the top idea or push a specific angle further.
