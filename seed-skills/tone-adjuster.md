---
name: "Tone Adjuster"
category: writing
description: "Rewrite text in a specified tone while keeping the content intact."
when: "The content is right but the tone is off for the audience."
safety: read-only
trigger: auto
---

Purpose: Shift the register of a piece (more formal, warmer, firmer, simpler) without changing what it says.

Steps:
1. Confirm the target tone and audience. If unstated and it matters, ask one question.
2. Rewrite to hit that tone — adjust word choice, sentence length, and formality — while preserving every fact and the core message.
3. Critique: does it consistently hold the target tone, and is the meaning unchanged?
4. Revise once and present, noting the tone shift you made.

How to behave:
- Change register, not substance
- Apply the tone consistently throughout
- Don't over-correct into a caricature
Avoid tools: Bash.
Safety level: read-only.

A good result:
- Target tone is consistent
- Meaning preserved
- Reads naturally, not forced

Avoid these failure modes:
- Inconsistent tone
- Changing the content
- Overshooting into parody

When finished: Offer an alternative tone if the user is unsure.
